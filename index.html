Shri Harbansh Singh Ji

A Tribute to a Loktantra Rakshak Senani

परिचय • Introduction

श्री हरबंश सिंह जी का जन्म 15 जुलाई 1945 को हुआ था। वह लोकतंत्र की रक्षा के लिए किए गए संघर्षों में एक अग्रणी सेनानी थे।

Shri Harbansh Singh Ji was born on 15 July 1945. He was a fearless activist and Loktantra Rakshak Senani who fought bravely during the Emergency and numerous people’s movements across India.

Life Journey • जीवन यात्रा

  • Participated in the Bharat Bandh movement as a minor and was jailed for 1 month.
  • In 1974, protested against Railway Minister Jagjivan Ram by blocking his helicopter landing.
  • Jailed in solitary confinement during the All India Railway Strike led by George Fernandes.
  • Imprisoned for 18 months during the 1975 Emergency; faced torture and isolation.
  • Jailed under Section 128 of the Railway Act during protests for gangman rights.
  • Led agitation against Ramnagar toll tax; jailed for 11 months.
  • And many more contributions in India's democratic history…
