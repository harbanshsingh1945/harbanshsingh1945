Tribute to Shri Harbansh Singh Ji

Loktantra Rakshak Senani | Born: 15 July 1947

Introduction

Shri Harbansh Singh Ji was a true Loktantra Rakshak Senani and a fearless patriot who actively stood for democracy during challenging times in India's political history. His strength, simplicity, and dedication inspired many and earned him deep respect in society. This site honors his life and legacy.

Speech and Tribute Video

Key Highlights of His Life

  • Born on 15 July 1947 in a respected family known for values and service.
  • Actively took part in the Loktantra Raksha movement during the Emergency.
  • Faced challenges with resilience, never compromising democratic ideals.
  • Known for his community leadership, wisdom, and inspiring personality.
  • Felicited by various organizations for his contributions to society.

Life Timeline

  • 1947: Birth of Shri Harbansh Singh Ji on 15th July.
  • 1975–77: Actively resisted Emergency policies as Loktantra Rakshak Senani.
  • 1980s–2000s: Continued social involvement and mentoring youth.
  • Later Years: Honored by citizens and civic bodies for lifelong service.
